What is this?

Factor of Safety is an upcoming digging/horror game featuring material physics. The player needs to dig in a terrain that can collapse at any time if the structural integrity fails.

Unlike the single player demo, the full release of Factor of Safety will be a multiplayer game release on Steam where groups of players will be tasked with completing various missions while avoiding danger.
